Your Privacy Matters
File Zipper is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with a privacy-first approach - all file operations happen locally on your device, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or files to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

File Zipper does NOT collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect usernames, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data
  • We do not track your browsing history or app usage
  • We do not access your contacts, photos (except files you explicitly select), or other personal content

2. How the App Works

2.1 Local Processing

All file compression and extraction operations are performed entirely on your device. Your files never leave your device and are not uploaded to any servers.

2.2 Permissions Required

File Zipper requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage Access: To read files you want to compress and to save extracted or compressed files to your device
  • Notifications: To inform you when compression or extraction tasks are complete (especially for large files processed in background)

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and nothing else.

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage Only

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • App Preferences: Your chosen language, theme (light/dark mode), and other settings
  • Operation History: A local record of compression and extraction operations for your convenience (file names, dates, sizes)
  • Cache: Temporary files created during compression/extraction processes

All this data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through the app's settings.

3.2 No Cloud Storage

File Zipper does not use any cloud storage services. Your files and operation history are never synchronized or backed up to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

File Zipper does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app operates completely offline and does not make any network requests, except for:

  • Checking for app updates (if you have enabled this in your device's app store settings)

5. Children's Privacy

File Zipper does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: View your operation history and settings within the app
  • Delete: Clear your history and cache through Settings â†’ Storage â†’ Clear History/Cache
  • Export: Your compressed files are stored in your chosen location on your device
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally using Android/iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • File operations use industry-standard compression libraries (Archive package)

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We will notify you of any material changes through the app.

Continued use of File Zipper after changes means you accept the updated privacy policy.

9. Open Source

File Zipper is built using open-source libraries and follows best practices for user privacy. The app's architecture ensures that your data never leaves your device.

10. Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
