Information Collection and Use

File Zipper does NOT collect any personally identifiable information. The app is designed to work entirely offline and all file operations are performed locally on your device.

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your app preferences (language selection, theme mode)
  • Operation history (list of compressed/extracted files for your convenience)
  • Temporary cache files created during compression/extraction processes

Your files and personal content never leave your device and are not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does use the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

What data does Firebase collect?

  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App usage data (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and error logs (to help us fix bugs)
  • Anonymous analytics data (no personally identifiable information)

Important: Your files and personal content are NEVER sent to Firebase. Only anonymous usage statistics and crash data are collected to improve app quality and fix bugs.

File Zipper does NOT use:

  • Location tracking
  • Access to contacts, camera, or microphone

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage Access: To read files you want to compress/extract and to save processed files to your device
  • Notifications: To inform you when compression or extraction tasks are complete

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality. We do not access, collect, or transmit your files or any personal data.

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Application, in case of an error in the app, we collect data and information through Firebase Crashlytics called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, crash logs, and other diagnostic information.

This data is collected automatically by Firebase Crashlytics and helps us:

  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Understand which devices and OS versions have issues

Important: Crash logs do NOT contain your files or personal content. They only contain technical information about what went wrong in the app.
